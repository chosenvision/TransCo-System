Company Overview

The National Transmission Corporation (TransCo) is a government-owned and controlled corporation responsible for the operation, maintenance, and development of the country's power transmission system.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, adequate, and economical transmission of electric power throughout the Philippines while ensuring the stability and security of the power grid.

Our Vision

To be the premier transmission utility in Southeast Asia, recognized for excellence in service delivery, operational efficiency, and sustainable development.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all our endeavors
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and methodologies
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmental stewardship
  • Service: We prioritize the needs of our stakeholders

Key Responsibilities

  • Operation and maintenance of transmission facilities
  • Planning and development of transmission infrastructure
  • Ensuring grid stability and reliability
  • Facilitating competitive electricity market
  • Compliance with regulatory requirements
